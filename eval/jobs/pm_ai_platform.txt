Senior Product Manager, AI Features — Synthos

About Synthos
Synthos builds the AI layer for knowledge workers — a Series C platform ($120M raised, 800K users) that lets teams create, search, and act on company knowledge using LLMs. Think Notion meets Copilot, built for mid-market companies.

The Role
We need a Senior PM to define and ship AI-powered features across our core product surface. You'll work closely with our ML engineers and the Anthropic/OpenAI APIs to ship features that genuinely change how users work — not AI theatre.

What you'll do
- Define the product vision for AI features: smart search, auto-summarisation, AI-assisted editing, agent workflows
- Collaborate with ML engineers to scope what's feasible given current model capabilities and latency constraints
- Own the full feature lifecycle: discovery, spec, build, eval, launch, iterate
- Design AI evaluation frameworks to measure output quality — not just engagement metrics
- Work cross-functionally with sales and CS to understand enterprise buyer needs
- Communicate clearly about AI limitations and model uncertainty to non-technical stakeholders

What we're looking for
Must-haves:
- 5+ years product management experience
- At least 1–2 years shipping AI or ML-powered features in a product role
- Ability to hold a technical conversation with ML engineers (you don't code, but you understand tradeoffs)
- Experience designing evaluation metrics for subjective outputs (LLM quality, retrieval relevance, etc.)
- Strong product instinct for when AI adds genuine value vs. when it adds complexity

Nice to have:
- Experience with RAG, embeddings, or prompt engineering in a product context
- Background in enterprise or B2B SaaS
- Experience at a company that ships to developers or technical users

Compensation: $160,000–$190,000 base + equity
Location: San Francisco or Remote (US/Europe)
Visa sponsorship: Yes
