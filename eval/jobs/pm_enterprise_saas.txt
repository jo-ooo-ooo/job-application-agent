Senior Product Manager — Meridian HR

About Meridian
Meridian is an enterprise HR platform (Series D, 2,000+ enterprise customers, 15M employees managed) used by Fortune 500 companies for workforce planning, compliance reporting, and benefits administration. We're a 10-year-old company modernising a legacy category.

The Role
We're hiring a Senior PM to own our compliance and reporting module — the product surface that enterprise HR directors and legal teams use to run mandatory regulatory reports (SOX, GDPR, EEOC). This is a high-stakes area where accuracy and auditability matter more than delight.

What you'll do
- Own the compliance reporting product area end-to-end: discovery, roadmap, delivery
- Work directly with enterprise customers (F500 HR directors, legal ops) in deep discovery sessions
- Partner with enterprise sales and solutions consultants to understand deal blockers and renewal risks
- Define detailed functional specs for complex rule-based business logic (regulatory formulae, audit trails)
- Manage a backlog across 3 engineering teams in a scaled Agile environment (SAFe)
- Present roadmap and trade-offs to executive stakeholders (CPO, CRO)
- Coordinate with external auditors and legal counsel on compliance requirements in 12 markets

What we're looking for
Must-haves:
- 6+ years product management experience, with at least 3 years in enterprise B2B SaaS
- Proven track record working with large enterprise customers (>5,000 employees) in a product role
- Experience owning complex rule-based or compliance-adjacent products
- Comfortable navigating large organisations and multiple internal stakeholders
- Experience with SAFe or large-scale Agile delivery frameworks

Nice to have:
- Domain experience in HR, payroll, or compliance software
- Experience in regulated industries (finance, healthcare, government)
- MBA or legal background

Compensation: $175,000–$205,000 base + bonus
Location: New York or Chicago (3 days/week in office required)
Visa sponsorship: No
